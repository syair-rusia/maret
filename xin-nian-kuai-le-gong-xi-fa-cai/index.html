Xin Xing Shi Cheng. Thus Gong Xi Fa Cai means wishing you to be prosperous in the coming year. Xin Nian Kuai Le Gong Xi Fa Cai. Some believe that the term Gong Xi Fa Cai is more focused on material gain than the spirit of togetherness so they prefer to call Xin Nian Kuai Le which means Happy New Year said Yam Kah Kean.
